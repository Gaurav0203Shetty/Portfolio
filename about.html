ABOUT ME

Beyond the world of coding and algorithms, I have a soaring enthusiasm for aviation photography.

Capturing the beauty of aircraft in flight and exploring the intricate details of aviation has become my creative escape.

Join me on this exciting journey as I navigate the skies of both engineering and photography!
